# Neighbors Helping Neighbors Charity Event 2026
## Preliminary Event Plan & Strategy

**Event Date:** November 2026 (venue TBD - same as prior year)  
**Expected Attendance:** 200+ members and guests  
**Fundraising Goal:** $150,000  
**Beneficiary:** Neighbors Helping Neighbors (501c3)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This plan leverages your existing 60+ active members and their business networks to execute a highly coordinated charity event targeting $150K in revenue through multiple streams: ticket sales, sponsorships, silent auction, 50/50 raffle, and direct donations.

## EVENT STRUCTURE & FORMAT

### Recommended Format: Gala Dinner + Silent Auction + Live Raffle

**Event Flow (Sample 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM)**
- **5:30 PM** — Silent auction preview, raffle ticket sales, cocktails
- **6:00 PM** — Welcome & opening remarks (5 min)
- **6:05 PM** — Dinner service begins
- **6:45 PM** — Live auctioneer presentation / appeal
- **7:00 PM** — Live raffle drawings (50/50, prize drawings)
- **7:30 PM** — Silent auction closes, winners announced
- **8:00 PM** — Dessert & networking / final donations
- **9:00 PM** — Wind down

## SILENT AUCTION STRATEGY

### Target: 30-40 Items

**Item Categories:**
- **Premium Experiences** (4-5 items): Golf outings, vacations, VIP events
- **Dining & Entertainment** (5-6 items): Restaurant packages, concert tickets, sports events
- **Services & Professional** (6-8 items): Professional services (legal, accounting, consulting), home services
- **Wellness & Lifestyle** (4-5 items): Spa packages, fitness memberships, personal training
- **Travel & Lodging** (3-4 items): Weekend getaways, hotel stays
- **Business & Merchandise** (4-5 items): Company products, gift baskets, tech items
- **Charitable Giving** (2-3 items): In-kind donations, matched donations

**Auction Item Recruitment Process:**
1. Personal asks from Auction Committee to members
2. Follow-up emails with item donation form
3. Item deadline: Sept 30
4. Catalog creation: Oct 1-15
5. Online bidding platform setup: Oct 15
6. Silent auction runs: Event night
