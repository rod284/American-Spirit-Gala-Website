## Daily Updates

### Ticket Sales (Update after each purchase)

**When a ticket is sold:**
1. Open the "Tickets" tab
2. Find the next empty row
3. Fill in:
   - Ticket #: T001, T002, etc. (sequential)
   - Date Purchased: Today's date
   - Attendee Name: Full name
   - Email: Contact email
   - Phone: Contact phone
   - Company: Company/organization
   - Dietary Restrictions: Note any allergies/preferences
   - Table Assignment: Leave blank for now (assign later)
   - Notes: Any special requests

**Update Summary tab:**
- "Tickets Sold To Date": Update count
- "Tickets Remaining": Subtract from 160
- "Total Revenue from Tickets": Sold count × $50

### Sponsorship Sales (Update as commitments come in)

**When a sponsorship is committed:**
1. Open the "Sponsorships" tab
2. Find the sponsorship tier row
3. Increment "Quantity Sold" by 1
4. Update "Total Revenue" column
5. Add sponsor details:
   - Company name
   - Contact person & email
   - Payment status (Pending/Paid)
   - Logo submission status
   - Table seats if applicable

**Update Summary:**
- Recalculate total sponsorship revenue
- Update "Sponsorship Goal %"

### Assignment Process
1. **Reserved tables first:** Sponsors with table reservations get branded table card
2. **Sponsorship tiers:** Captain's Helm, Band Sponsor, Captain's Feast get premium seating
3. **Open tables:** First-come, first-served
4. **Group requests:** Honor requests to sit together
5. **VIP/committee:** Assign to best viewing tables

### Table Numbering
- Tables 1-5: Premium (near stage, best view)
- Tables 6-10: Standard (center)
- Tables 11-15: Bar/lounge area
- Tables 16-20: Auction area

## Tips for Success

✅ **DO:**
- Update daily if possible (even just one field)
- Use consistent data entry (names, emails, phone format)
- Flag issues immediately (payment pending, missing info)
- Back up spreadsheet weekly
- Share updates with committee
- Celebrate milestones (150 tickets, $30K raised, etc.)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Delete rows (just mark as inactive if needed)
- Change formulas without understanding them
- Leave pending items unresolved
- Wait until last minute to follow up
- Forget to confirm dietary restrictions
- Assume attendees remember their tickets
