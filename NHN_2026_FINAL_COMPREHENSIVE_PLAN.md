- Networking

- High-energy bidding
- Creates momentum for silent auction

### 7:30 PM — 50/50 Raffle Drawing
- Live raffle drawings for multiple prizes
- Build excitement
- Announce 50/50 winner (announce $5K prize)
- Encourage more raffle ticket purchases

### 8:00 PM — Dessert & Live Giving Moment
- Dessert service
- Partner organization testimonial (different partner)
- Share collective impact: "200,000 meals. 8 ramps. 150 kids at camp."
- **LIVE GIVING ASK** — Pledge cards at tables
  - Target: $5,000-10,000 in live pledges
  - Read pledges aloud to recognize generosity
  - Thank-you recognition on the spot

### 8:30 PM — Silent Auction Closes
- Final call for silent auction bidding
- Announce top bidders & winners
- High bidders receive recognition
- Settlement details provided

### 8:45 PM — Final Remarks & Raffle
- Event chair thanks & closing remarks
- Final raffle drawings
- Announce total raised (if possible)
- Invite to next year's event

### 9:00 PM — Event Winds Down
- Guests depart with auction items/receipts
- Volunteers help with checkout
