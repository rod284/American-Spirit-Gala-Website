## MESSAGING SHIFT (Critical!)

### OLD MESSAGING:
"Sponsorship opportunities at multiple levels. We have Platinum ($9K), Gold ($3K), and Silver ($1K) tiers."

### NEW MESSAGING:
"Be a presenting sponsor and name the entertainment. Sponsor the yacht rock band, the signature cocktail, the photo booth, or the welcome experience. Options from $500-$15,000. Every dollar visible. Every sponsorship creates lasting impression."

---

## WHY THIS WORKS BETTER

✅ **Specific Benefits** — Companies know EXACTLY what they're sponsoring  
✅ **Visible ROI** — Logo literally everywhere on that element (hat, bar, photos, etc.)  
✅ **"Presented By" Language** — Creates prestige comparable to major events  
✅ **Tangible Deliverables** — Sponsor owns a specific part of the event  
✅ **Accessible Price Points** — $1.5K-$5K options (more companies can participate)  
✅ **Photo/Social Gold** — Branded items generate organic social media buzz  
✅ **Memorable** — "We sponsored the yacht rock band!" vs. "We were a gold sponsor"  
✅ **Scalable** — Add sponsorships as you identify matches

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS

### Before Launch:
1. Update website with NEW sponsorship descriptions (specific, tangible benefits)
2. Update sponsorship deck with "Presented By" language
3. Identify which sponsorships to target with which companies

### During Outreach:
1. Lead with most visible (Hat Sponsor, Band Sponsor, Bar Captain, Photo Booth)
2. Use specific language: "Your company's logo will be on 160 branded hats all night"
3. Show examples of visibility (mockups of branded items, signage, etc.)

### At Event:
1. Over-deliver on visibility promises
2. Make sure every sponsor sees their brand at the event
3. Document with photos for social media
