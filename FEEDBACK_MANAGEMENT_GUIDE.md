# Feedback Management Guide - Suggestions, Comments & Questions

## 🎯 Overview

A new **Suggestions, Comments & Questions** section on your website allows visitors to:
- ✅ Ask questions about the event
- ✅ Provide suggestions for improvements
- ✅ Submit feedback and comments
- ✅ Express sponsorship interest
- ✅ Donate auction items
- ✅ Volunteer for the event
- ✅ Opt-in to event updates

All feedback is automatically emailed to Rod Dennis with a CC to the sender.

---

## 📍 Location on Website

**Position:** Right before the footer, after all event sections

**Access:** Scroll down to see section titled: **"💬 Suggestions, Comments & Questions"**

---

## 🎨 Form Fields

### **Required Fields (*):**
1. **Your Name** - Full name of person submitting
2. **Email Address** - Contact email for response
3. **Category** - Dropdown with 8 options (see below)
4. **Message** - Detailed text of feedback/question

### **Optional Fields:**
- **Email Updates Checkbox** - Opt-in to event updates list

## 📧 How Feedback is Delivered

### **Email Routing:**
```

Body includes:
├─ Submitter's name
├─ Submitter's email
├─ Category selected
├─ Whether they opted in for updates
├─ Their full message
└─ Timestamp of submission
```

## 🎓 Common Questions About the Form

**Q: What happens when someone submits feedback?**
A: An email is automatically sent to rod@roddennis.com and CC'd to the submitter. Rod can then forward to the appropriate team member.

**Q: Can people opt-in to email updates?**
A: Yes! There's a checkbox. Anyone who checks it should be added to the event updates email list.

**Q: What if someone has an urgent question?**
A: Quick answers are provided in the form itself. For urgent matters, they can call Rod at 480-695-0733.

**Q: Is feedback moderated before being sent?**
A: No, it goes directly to Rod's email. He reviews and responds.

**Q: Can we export the feedback data?**
A: Currently it comes via email. You can maintain a spreadsheet log to track all submissions.
