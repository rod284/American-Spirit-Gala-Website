# All Hands on Deck 2026 — Competitive Analysis
## Saguaros of Scottsdale vs. Celebrity Fight Night Model
### Applying Best Practices to NHN Gala with $50 Ticket Price

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After analyzing the Saguaros of Scottsdale charity organization (35 years, multi-event model) and Celebrity Fight Night (30 years, $90M+ raised), here are key insights:

**Key Finding:** The Saguaros use **specific, tangible sponsorships** (shirt sponsor, hat sponsor, swag bag sponsor) at mid-tier prices ($3K-$10K) rather than generic "Gold/Silver" tiers. This creates highly visible, memorable brand associations.

**Application for NHN:** Restructure your sponsorship tiers from generic (Captain's Helm, Crew Chief) to **specific, nautical-themed, tangible sponsorships** (Yacht Rock Band Sponsor, Captain's Hat Sponsor, Bar Captain, etc.)

## THE SAGUAROS OF SCOTTSDALE — ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW

**Mission:** Children's charities across Arizona  
**Operating Since:** ~35 years  
**Events:** Multiple annual fundraisers (NiteFlite, Olympiad, Brokers for Kids)  
**Annual Reach:** Hundreds of thousands raised across events  

### Key Success Factors:
1. **Multiple events throughout year** — Diversified revenue streams
2. **Themed sponsorships** — Specific, tangible benefits (not generic)
3. **Tiered entry points** — From $100 tickets to $25K sponsorships
4. **Corporate team involvement** — Companies bring teams/groups
5. **Community reputation** — 35 years of consistent execution

## CELEBRITY FIGHT NIGHT — THE POWERHOUSE MODEL

**Event History:** 30 years, raised $90M+ for charity  
**Location:** Phoenix, Arizona  
**Format:** Celebrity boxing exhibition + fundraising gala  
**Beneficiary:** Cancer research (Gateway for Cancer Research, Kirk Gibson Foundation)  

### Key Insights:

**What Made Fight Night Legendary:**
1. **Celebrity draw** — Charles Barkley, Dan Majerle, Michael Carbajal involved from start
2. **Combat spectacle** — Live boxing entertainment (not typical gala)
3. **Major fundraising** — $90M+ over 30 years = ~$3M per year average
4. **Evolved model** — Started as sports-centered, evolved to celebrity-entertainment-centered
5. **Long-term commitment** — 30 years of consistent execution = trust & community buy-in

### Fight Night Structure:
- **Live Boxing Matches** — Celebrity/pro fighters, audience excitement
- **Silent Auction** — High-value items (art, experiences, memorabilia)
- **Live Auction** — Premium items with celebrity auctioneers creating bidding wars
- **Dinner/Gala** — High-end catering, entertainment, VIP experiences
- **Raffle** — Lower price point participation

### CURRENT NHN APPROACH (Generic):
- Platinum Sponsor — $9,000
- Gold Sponsor — $3,000

**Specific, Nautical-Themed Sponsorships:**

| Sponsorship | Price | Tangible Benefit | Visibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Yacht Rock Band Sponsor** | $5,000 | Event named after you ("Presented by [Co.]") | Band intro from stage |
| **Captain's Hat & Gear Sponsor** | $2,500 | Logo on 160+ branded hats guests wear all night | Every guest photo |
| **Bar Captain (Beverage)** | $3,500 | Custom cocktail named after you + bar signage | Every drink order all night |
| **Captain's Feast (Food) Sponsor** | $4,000 | "Dinner Presented by [Co.]" + food station signage | Logo at food all evening |
| **Photo Booth Sponsor** | $2,000 | Logo on all 160 photos guests share on social media | Every photo published |
| **Raffle Prize Sponsor** | $2,000 | "Raffle Presented by [Co.]" + prize announcements | Every raffle drawing |
| **Silent Auction Sponsor** | $1,500 | "Silent Auction Presented by [Co.]" + display signage | Auction item descriptions |
| **Welcome Lanyard Sponsor** | $2,000 | Logo on 160 lanyards guests wear + event name | Every guest has your logo |
| **Event Program Sponsor** | $1,000 | Back cover ad in program + website mention | Guests hold/read program |

### Why This Works Better:

✅ **Visible & Tangible** — Sponsors see their brand in action, not just in program
✅ **Memorable** — "We sponsored the yacht rock band!" resonates more than "Platinum"
✅ **Photo Worthy** — Branded hats, lanyards, photos = organic social media
✅ **Mid-Tier Accessible** — $1.5K-$5K range reaches more companies than $9K tier
✅ **Creates Prestige** — "Presented by" language elevates sponsor status
✅ **Scalable** — Add sponsors as you identify matching companies

## FIGHT NIGHT MODEL APPLICATIONS FOR NHN

### Lesson #1: AMPLIFY THE ENTERTAINMENT
**Fight Night Model:** Live boxing = exciting, differentiating spectacle  
**NHN Application:** Yacht Rock Band + Live Auctioneer + DJ = entertainment-focused evening

Your silent auction + live band + raffle is already strong. Enhance with:
- **Professional auctioneer** (create excitement for live auction)
- **Live band performing throughout** (not just background)
- **Interactive elements** (photo booth, games, surprise announcements)

### Lesson #2: CREATE CELEBRITY/HIGH-VALUE DRAW
**Fight Night Model:** Celebrity fighters created prestige & ticket demand  
**NHN Application:** Recruit local celebrity/influencer to MC, donate item, create buzz

Suggestions for Phoenix:
- Local news anchor or personality as MC
- Phoenix sports figure to present raffle or live auction
- Celebrity chef for gala dinner reveal

### Lesson #3: TIERED BIDDING STRATEGY
**Fight Night Model:** Silent auction + live auction + raffle = multiple entry points

**Your Model (Already Strong):**
- Silent auction ($100-$500 bids) — Accessible entry
- Live auction ($1000-$5000+) — Premium spectacle
- 50/50 raffle ($20 tickets) — Impulse participation
- Direct donations ($5-$50) — Last-minute giving

Keep this structure. Fight Night validates multi-tiered approach.

### Lesson #4: CORPORATE TEAM PARTICIPATION
**Saguaros Model:** Teams of employees compete/participate together

**NHN Application Options:**
1. **Table sponsorships** — Teams of 8-10 from company sit together
2. **Auction item donations** — Encourage corporate teams to donate items
3. **Volunteer teams** — Companies sponsor volunteers as group
4. **Raffle team challenges** — Corporate teams pool raffle tickets to compete

## PRICING STRATEGY WITH $50 TICKET ENTRY

**Important Finding:** Saguaros events use tiered ticket/sponsorship pricing:
- Olympiad: $100 tickets, $3K-$10K sponsorships (30:1 ratio)
- NiteFlite: ~$100-200 gala tickets, $3K-$25K sponsorships

**Your Model:** $50 tickets, $500-$15K sponsorships

**Recommendation:** Keep $50 tickets but emphasize sponsorships as PRIMARY revenue:

**Revised Revenue Targets:**
```
Sponsorships (15-20 sponsors)          $75,000-100,000  ← FOCUS HERE
Tickets (160 @ $50)                      $8,000
Auction (35-40 items)                   $30,000
Raffle (500 tickets @ $20)              $10,000
Direct donations                         $5,000-10,000
─────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL                                 $128,000-163,000
```

**Key Insight:** Sponsorships should be 50-60% of revenue, not tickets. Price tickets accessibly ($50) to get bodies in room, but make sponsorship the fundraising workhorse.

## RECOMMENDATIONS FOR ALL HANDS ON DECK 2026

### IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

1. **Restructure Sponsorships** (before launch)
   - Move from generic "Platinum/Gold/Silver" to specific, tangible sponsorships
   - Example: Instead of "Sponsor for $5K (generic tier)" → "Yacht Rock Band Sponsor for $5K"
   - Use Saguaros model: Create visible, memorable benefits

2. **Create "Presented By" Language**
   - Instead of "Gold Sponsor Acme Corp"
   - Use "Yacht Rock Band Presented by Acme Corp" or "Dinner Presented by Acme Corp"
   - Creates prestige and clear value association

3. **Add Tangible Touchpoints**
   - Branded hats (like Saguaros Olympiad shirt/hat sponsorships)
   - Branded lanyards
   - Branded cocktail names
   - Branded program/signage everywhere
   - Photo booth branded items

4. **Emphasize Sponsorships as Revenue Driver**
   - Keep $50 tickets (accessible, volume play)
   - Make sponsorships $75K-$100K goal (primary revenue)
   - Use Saguaros tiering: $1K-$5K mid-tier options (more accessible than $9K tier)

5. **Add Entertainment Elements** (Fight Night lesson)
   - Professional live auctioneer (create excitement)
   - DJ + live band (not just background music)
   - Celebrity MC or guest personality
   - Interactive elements (photo booth, games)

6. **Corporate Team Focus**
   - Encourage companies to sponsor as teams
   - Market to corporate groups (not just individuals)
   - Create team-building angles (Saguaros Olympiad model)
