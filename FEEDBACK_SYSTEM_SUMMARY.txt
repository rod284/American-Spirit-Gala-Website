===============================================================
📧 HOW FEEDBACK IS DELIVERED
===============================================================

1. VISITOR SUBMITS FORM
   ├─ Fills in name, email, category, message
   └─ Clicks "📨 Send Feedback"

2. SYSTEM SENDS EMAIL
   ├─ TO: rod@roddennis.com
   ├─ CC: Submitter's email (confirmation)
   ├─ Subject: "Feedback - [Category Name]"
   └─ Body: Complete submission details

3. ROD REVIEWS
   ├─ Reads incoming feedback
   ├─ Forwards to appropriate team member
   └─ Logs in tracking spreadsheet

4. TEAM MEMBER RESPONDS
   ├─ Replies to submitter within 24 hours
   └─ Updates feedback log with response date
