## DONORBOX ADVANTAGES FOR YOUR GALA

✅ **Existing donor database** — Your current donors can be invited to gala
✅ **Campaign tracking** — Create gala-specific campaign to track all revenue streams
✅ **Multiple payment methods** — Credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, bank transfer
✅ **Recurring giving** — Offer monthly support for partner organizations
✅ **Donor management** — Segment donors, track engagement, generate reports
✅ **Customizable forms** — Create gala-specific donation pages & ticket sales

### 2. CREATE SUB-CAMPAIGNS FOR EACH REVENUE STREAM

**This allows tracking WHERE money comes from:**

`https://donorbox.org/nhn-2026-gala-sponsorships`
- **Donation levels:**
  - $1,000 — Silver Sponsorship
  - $3,000 — Gold Sponsorship
  - $9,000 — Platinum Sponsorship
- **Description:** "Sponsor the 2026 NHN Gala and gain brand visibility + leadership platform"
- **Confirmation message:** "Our sponsorship coordinator will contact you within 24 hours to confirm benefits"

`https://donorbox.org/nhn-2026-gala-donations`

```

$500 Supports: Materials for one accessibility ramp (Rebuilding Together)
```

**Individual Partner Pages:**
- `https://donorbox.org/nhn-gala-camp-kesem`

- Provides data: "Which partner resonates most with our donors?"

---

### 4. RECURRING GIVING OPTION (MULTIPLY IMPACT)

**Offer this on every gala page:**

"Want to make an ongoing impact? Consider a monthly gift to NHN's work with [Partner Organization]"

**Preset monthly amounts:**
- $25/month — Sustaining Friend
- $50/month — Community Builder
- $100/month — Impact Partner

**Why Include:**
- Some donors will convert to recurring (lifetime value multiplier)
- Creates sustainable funding for partners
- Shows annual commitment in reports (impressive to sponsors)

## EVENT DAY DONORBOX STRATEGY

### 1. ON-SITE QR CODES

**Print QR codes at each table linking to:**
- `https://donorbox.org/nhn-2026-gala-donations` (General donations)
- Or specific partner pages if you want to feature one partner per table

**Printed materials:**
- 20-30 table cards with QR code + "Scan to donate"
- Small cards at silent auction checkout
- Raffle ticket sales station

**Why QR:** Mobile guests can donate instantly without cash/check

### 2. PLEDGE CARDS AT TABLES

**During dessert (8:00-9:00 PM) — "Live Giving Moment"**

Use Donorbox to track these:
- Partner verbally pledges amount
- You read pledge aloud ("Thank you [Name] for $X!")
- They receive card to fill out with contact/amount
- Scan card or manually enter into Donorbox after event
- Send thank-you email via Donorbox within 24 hours

**Goal:** $5,000-10,000 in live pledges through this moment

### 3. MOBILE FUNDRAISING

**If you have fundraising volunteers:**
- Volunteers can access Donorbox on tablets/phones at event
- Share link verbally: "Use your phone to donate now"
- Follow up: "Would you like to make a pledge? I can process it right now"

## DONORBOX + AUCTION INTEGRATION

### SILENT AUCTION CHECKOUT

**If using online silent auction platform (22Auctions, etc.):**

After auction winner is announced:
1. Guest pays for item via 22Auctions OR Donorbox
2. Payment confirmed & receipt sent
3. Item delivery arranged
4. Transaction appears in Donorbox "Auction Revenue" campaign

**Benefit:** All payments in one place, easy reconciliation

### RAFFLE INTEGRATION

**If selling raffle tickets online:**
- Create Donorbox campaign: "50/50 Raffle Tickets - $20 each"
- Track raffle revenue separately
- Split confirmed at end: 50% to NHN, 50% to winner
