FEEDBACK & SUGGESTIONS MANAGEMENT - QUICK REFERENCE
All Hands on Deck Event
===============================================================

🎯 WHAT IS IT?
A new section on your website where visitors can submit:
├─ Questions about the event
├─ Suggestions for improvements
├─ Feedback and comments
├─ Sponsorship interests
├─ Auction item donations
├─ Volunteer offers
└─ General inquiries

===============================================================
⚡ HOW IT WORKS
===============================================================

VISITOR FILLS FORM:
├─ Name
├─ Email
├─ Category (dropdown with 8 options)
├─ Message
└─ Optional: Opt-in for email updates

↓

SYSTEM SENDS EMAIL:
├─ TO: rod@roddennis.com
├─ CC: Submitter's email (so they see it was sent)
└─ Subject: "Feedback - [Category Name]"

↓

ROD REVIEWS & RESPONDS:
├─ Within 24 hours ideally
├─ Forwards to appropriate team member
└─ Logs in feedback spreadsheet

===============================================================
📍 WHERE ON WEBSITE?
===============================================================

Location: Right before footer
Scroll: All the way down to find it
Label: "💬 Suggestions, Comments & Questions"

Quick access:
https://allhandsondecknhn.netlify.app/#suggestions

===============================================================
📋 FEEDBACK CATEGORIES
===============================================================

When someone clicks "Category" dropdown:

1️⃣ 💡 Suggestion for Event
   → Ideas to improve the event

2️⃣ ❓ Question About Event
   → General event questions

3️⃣ ❓ How to Participate Question
   → Questions about tickets, sponsorships, auctions

4️⃣ 🤝 Interest in Sponsorship
   → Companies interested in sponsoring
   → RESPOND ASAP (within 2 hours if possible)

5️⃣ 🎁 Auction Item Donation
   → People wanting to donate auction items
   → RESPOND WITHIN 4 HOURS

6️⃣ 🙋 Volunteer Interest
   → People wanting to help at the event

7️⃣ 📝 Feedback/Comment
   → General feedback or comments

8️⃣ 💬 Other
   → Anything else
